What is the INR calculator?
The INR calculator is a tool that determines both the extrinsic pathway and the common pathway of coagulation.
How to calculate INR?
To calculate INR you should use the following formula:
INR = (patient's PT / control PT) ^ ISI
Why use INR calculator?
The INR calculator is used in order to calculate both the extrinsic pathway and the common pathway of coagulation.
